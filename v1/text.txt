Artificial intelligence is transforming the way people interact with technology. Large language models learn patterns from text and generate meaningful responses based on context. Machine learning algorithms improve through training on diverse datasets. Neural networks consist of multiple layers that process information efficiently. Natural language processing enables computers to understand human language. Tokenization converts text into smaller units called tokens. Each token receives a unique numerical identifier from the vocabulary. Unknown words are replaced with a special unknown token. Padding tokens are added to sequences so they have equal length during training. The model predicts the next token by learning relationships between previous tokens. During optimization the loss function measures prediction errors and gradient descent updates the model parameters.
